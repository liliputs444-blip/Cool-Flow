The Band

We are Cool Flow, a student band from Rosemont Hills Montessori College in Danao City, Cebu. Hailing from the beautiful island of Cebu, we bring passion and harmony to every performance. We regularly perform at resto-bars and bring a special touch to weddings and private parties.

What We Do

Resto Bars

Live acoustic sessions to set the perfect mood for your evening.

Weddings

Making your special day unforgettable with personalized music.

Parties

High-energy performances for birthdays and special occasions.

Our Vision

To showcase the musical talent of Danao City and share our love for music across the Philippines, one performance at a time.
